Blissful Kids Clinic

Because every child deserves Blissful health.

Welcome to Blissful Kids Clinic, where your child's health, development, and well-being are our highest priority. Led by Dr. Sagar S, MD Paediatrics, we offer expert, evidence-based care in a nurturing environment designed to make both children and parents feel comfortable and supported.

Our clinic is dedicated to providing comprehensive pediatric care for children from infancy through adolescence, ensuring that every child receives the best possible start in life. We offer routine health check-ups, immunizations, and treatment for common and chronic illnesses. Our services also include growth and nutrition guidance and parenting support
